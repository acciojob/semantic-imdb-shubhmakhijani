Summary

Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a skilled thief who commits corporate espionage by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He's offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform the seemingly impossible: inception, the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious. Cobb and his team of specialists plan to pull off the reverse heist, but unforeseen dangers await as Cobb's own tragic past threatens to sabotage the mission.
